Design Review: Core Gameplay (1 of 4)

Three mechanics that transform how the game plays

1. Curve Shot Mechanic

How It Works

Input: Hold spacebar (desktop) or touch-hold (mobile) while the ball is in flight

Effect: A lateral force is applied to the ball in the direction the paddle is moving. Max ~15-degree deflection over the ball's travel.

Visual: A faint curved laser-sight line projects from the ball showing the bent trajectory while input is held. The line uses the ball's glow color at low opacity.

Tuning: Force is proportional to paddle velocity. Stationary paddle = no curve. Fast paddle movement = max curve. This means you steer by moving the paddle in the direction you want the ball to bend.

Why it matters: Every shot becomes intentional. Players can thread balls into gaps, target specific bricks, and aim for boss weak points.

2. Paddle Abilities (Cooldown-Based)

Three Abilities — One Per Level Set
⬆

PULSE

Shockwave blasts upward in a cone, damaging bricks in range. Great for clearing stubborn clusters. Levels 1-5.

🧲

MAGNET

Ball sticks to paddle on next contact. Aim with position + curve shot, release on click/tap. Levels 6-10.

⚡

PHASE SHIFT

Paddle instantly teleports to ball's X position. Emergency save. Visual: neon streak trail from old to new position. Levels 11+.

Activation: Press 'E' key (desktop) or double-tap (mobile)

Cooldown: 10 seconds. Neon ring around paddle fills as it charges. Glows bright when ready.

Boss fights: Boss drops a free Magnet power-up at each phase transition — a catch-and-aim reward moment.

3. Prism Spectrum Split (New Power-Up)

Rainbow Destruction
● ● ● ● ● ● ●

Trigger: Rare 5th power-up drop (rainbow diamond icon, ~8% drop rate)

Effect: Ball splits into 7 smaller balls, one per brick row color

Bonus: Color-matched ball vs brick = instant kill regardless of HP. Matched kills guarantee a power-up drop.

Duration: 8 seconds, then mini-balls fade. Main ball continues.

Synergy: This creates the most screenshot-worthy moments in the game — 7 colored balls with trails blazing through matching bricks, chain explosions everywhere. Auto Screenshot will love this.
