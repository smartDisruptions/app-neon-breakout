Milestone moments and reasons to come back

8. Sentinel Boss Fights

Every 5 Levels — A Living Geometric Entity

Structure

The boss is a large wireframe geometry (triangle at level 5, hexagon at level 10, eye-shape at level 15) floating in the upper half of the screen. It has 3-5 core nodes — glowing circles connected by neon lines. Outer nodes must be destroyed before inner ones become vulnerable.

Phases

Phase 1: Open

Boss drifts laterally. Outer nodes exposed. Ball bounces off the wireframe shell but damages nodes on direct hits. 2-3 outer nodes to destroy.

Phase 2: Aggro

Outer nodes gone. Boss speeds up, periodically drops hazard bricks (single bricks that fall toward paddle — hit them or dodge). Inner nodes exposed intermittently behind rotating shield segments.

Phase 3: Core

Final node. Boss pulses and shakes. Drops hazard bricks faster. Core is always exposed but boss moves erratically. Destroy it for a massive particle cascade + screen shake + bonus points.

Key Details

  • Phase transitions: Boss freezes, geometry unfolds/reconfigures with a neon flash. Drops a free Magnet power-up so the player can catch and aim.
  • Hazard bricks: Single falling bricks. If they reach the paddle line, they don't kill you — they shrink the paddle temporarily (3 seconds). Punishing but not lethal.
  • Curve Shot synergy: Players can curve the ball to thread past shield segments and hit exposed nodes. Skill-rewarding.
  • Boss HP scales: Level 5 boss has 3 nodes (easy intro). Level 10 has 4 nodes + faster shields. Level 15+ has 5 nodes + more hazard bricks.
  • Victory reward: Bonus life + score multiplier for next 3 levels.

9. Achievement Constellation

Star Map Trophy Room

COMBO HUNTER

Reach 15x combo

● Earned   ○ Locked   ─ Connected

Access: "ACHIEVEMENTS" button on title screen, or 'A' key.

Interaction: Hover/tap any star to see name + requirement. Earned stars show completion date.

Shape: The full constellation forms a paddle shape. As you earn more, the image emerges.

Storage: localStorage — persists between sessions.

Example Achievements (15-20 total)

● First Blood — Destroy your first brick
● Combo Hunter — Reach 15x combo
● Untouchable — Clear a level with no lives lost
● Sentinel Slayer — Defeat your first boss
● Prism Master — Destroy 20 bricks with one Prism
● Curve Ace — Hit 3 bricks in one curved shot
● Pulse Destroyer — Destroy 5 bricks with one Pulse
● Marathon — Reach level 20
